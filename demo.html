Step 4

Charge (Cycle 1)

Anyone can call charge() once the billing interval has elapsed. USDC flows from client to agent owner.

Step 5

Charge (Cycle 2)

Wait another interval and charge again. The cycle count increments with each successful charge.

Step 6

Cancel Subscription

Only the subscriber can cancel. No further charges will succeed after cancellation.
